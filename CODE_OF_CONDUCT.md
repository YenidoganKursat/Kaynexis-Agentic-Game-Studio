# Code Of Conduct

This repository expects respectful, direct, and collaborative behavior.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in feedback and disagreement
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person
- Share enough context for others to reproduce or understand the issue
- Prefer curiosity and evidence over blame

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- sharing private information without consent
- hostile or dismissive communication that blocks collaboration

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict access for repeated or severe violations.
