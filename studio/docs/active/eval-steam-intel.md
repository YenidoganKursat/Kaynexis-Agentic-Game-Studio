# Eval Plan: Steam Intel

## Change under test

- New Steam intel guide, example, research note, checklist, and routing surface for store traffic, wishlist, forum, hardware, and survey-backed market signals.

## Goal

- The repo should help the agent answer Steam signal questions with official sources first, a clear date window, a simple chart pack, and one short repo implication.

## Risks

- Steam traffic and wishlist data can be misread as the same thing.
- Forum themes can be mistaken for hard metrics.
- Currentness can drift if the report window is not stated.
- The user-facing output can become too wide unless the chart pack is kept small.

## Validation

- Run the route surface for a Steam signal prompt.
- Run the checklist surface for the same prompt.
- Confirm the docs validator accepts the new guide, example, research note, checklist, and eval plan.
- Confirm the repo layout validator knows the new files.

## Success criteria

- The router surfaces the Steam intel lane for Steam traffic, wishlist, forum, or hardware prompts.
- The checklist bundle includes the Steam intel discipline and its supporting sector / marketing / research layers.
- The example shows a small chart pack and a one-paragraph recommendation.
- The research note names the current window and the source family.
