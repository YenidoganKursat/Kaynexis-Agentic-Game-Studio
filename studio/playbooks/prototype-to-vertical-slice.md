# Playbook — Prototype to Vertical Slice

Move from proof-of-concept to one convincing, polished, repeatable slice.

## Phase sequence
- Confirm pillars
- Choose one slice
- Cut broad scope
- Lock quality bar
- Validate through playtests

## Recommended skills
- $project-radar
- $scope-rescue
- $feature-brief
- $gameplay-slice
- $qa-matrix
- $release-gate

## Exit criteria
- One polished slice
- documented risks
- repeatable build/test path
