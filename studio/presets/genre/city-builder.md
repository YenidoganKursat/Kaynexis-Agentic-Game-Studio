# Genre Preset — City Builder

City-scale simulation, zoning and service planning, transport or pathing bottlenecks, readable overlays, and long-session state management.

## Default assumptions
- Many systems interact at once
- The player needs debugging tools as much as they need content
- Simulation clarity matters more than flashy micro-interactions
- Long sessions and save trust are core to the genre

## Must-watch risks
- Simulation opacity making failure feel random
- Transport or pathing cost exploding with scale
- Overlays and UI hiding the real bottleneck
- Save/load drift in long-running cities
