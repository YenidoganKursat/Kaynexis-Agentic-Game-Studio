# Genre Preset — Platformer

Precise movement, fair readability, camera support, and cadence between mastery and rest.

## Default assumptions
- Movement feel is king
- Collision fairness matters
- Level readability must support flow

## Must-watch risks
- Input latency
- Ambiguous landing spaces
- Overlong checkpoints
- Camera frustration
