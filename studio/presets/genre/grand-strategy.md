# Genre Preset — Grand Strategy

Long-horizon realm planning, diplomacy, event pacing, layered map state, and campaign-scale persistence.

## Default assumptions
- The player thinks in turns, years, or eras rather than seconds
- UI must show why state changed, not just that it changed
- Events and diplomacy are core pacing tools
- Saves must remain trustworthy over very long campaigns

## Must-watch risks
- UI or map density becoming unreadable
- Diplomacy or events feeling arbitrary
- AI behavior being opaque or impossible to anticipate
- Save/state drift over long sessions
