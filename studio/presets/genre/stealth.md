# Genre Preset — Stealth

Patrol readability, suspicion states, visibility and noise rules, objective routing, and safe experimentation after detection.

## Default assumptions
- The player is reading systems before acting
- Detection rules must be learnable and fair
- Level geometry and props are part of the mechanic set
- Failure should teach rather than punish blindly

## Must-watch risks
- Detection feeling arbitrary
- Patrol or guard routes being too hard to parse
- Visibility or sound rules being unclear
- Retry loops becoming too punishing
