# Genre Preset — Auto-Battler

Draft or shop economy, small-board placement, automatic combat resolution, synergy discovery, and readable round outcomes.

## Default assumptions
- The player plans before combat rather than during it
- Board state and round logs must be very readable
- Economy and pool health matter as much as unit strength
- Build identity emerges through repeated adaptation

## Must-watch risks
- Shop or roster pools becoming opaque
- Board rules or synergies becoming unreadable
- Combat logs hiding why a round was won or lost
- Balance drift making the best build too dominant
