# Genre Preset — Life Sim

Character creation, household state, routine pacing, relationships, customization, and long-term identity or place attachment.

## Default assumptions
- Routine and identity matter more than twitch action
- Many state systems persist across days or weeks
- UI must explain the player's life changes clearly
- Content variety is a major production cost

## Must-watch risks
- Routine turning into chores
- Relationship state becoming opaque
- Content throughput failing to keep pace with player expectations
- Save corruption or calendar drift across long sessions
